# Evaluation Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing any answer.

## Content Checks

- The response is based on the full news text, not only the headline
- The central event is correct
- Important names, dates, and numbers match the source
- No outside facts were added

## Structure Checks

- Section 1 is a short simple summary
- Section 2 lists the main points
- Section 3 explains impact only when supported by the article
- Section 4 defines difficult terms in simple Marathi
- Section 5 stays neutral and factual

## Quality Checks

- The explanation is easy to scan
- The wording is simple enough for a general audience
- Quotes are not presented as confirmed facts
- Ambiguity is stated clearly instead of guessed
- The tone remains neutral
