# Output Guide

This skill should keep the explanation simple, factual, and easy to scan.

## Section 1: बातमीचा सोपा सारांश

Write 2-4 short sentences that explain the whole story in plain Marathi.

## Section 2: मुख्य मुद्दे

Use bullets for:
- the central event
- the main people or organizations involved
- the most important outcome or update

## Section 3: याचा लोकांवर परिणाम

Explain practical impact only if the article supports it.

Good examples:
- what this means for citizens
- what this means for workers or customers
- what this means for policy, prices, services, or safety

If the article does not support a clear impact, say that the effect is not yet clear.

## Section 4: कठीण शब्दांचे अर्थ

Define terms like:
- legal terms
- economic terms
- government terms
- technical terms

Keep the explanation short and in simple Marathi.

## Section 5: निष्पक्ष स्पष्टीकरण

Restate the story neutrally.

Rules:
- separate facts from opinions
- mention uncertainty when needed
- avoid emotional framing
- do not add a verdict unless the article itself supports one
