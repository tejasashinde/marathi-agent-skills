# Marathi News Simplifier

**Version:** 1.0

This skill turns difficult news text into simple, neutral Marathi.

## What it does

- Summarizes news in simple Marathi
- Lists the main points
- Explains likely impact on people
- Defines difficult words and terms
- Keeps the explanation neutral and factual

## When to use it

Use this skill when someone asks to:

- simplify a Marathi news article
- explain a news report in plain Marathi
- summarize a difficult current-affairs story
- understand the effect of a news event on people

## Output

The response should follow these five sections:

1. बातमीचा सोपा सारांश
2. मुख्य मुद्दे
3. याचा लोकांवर परिणाम
4. कठीण शब्दांचे अर्थ
5. निष्पक्ष स्पष्टीकरण

## Notes

- Do not add outside facts
- Do not take sides
- Do not turn news into opinion
- If the article is incomplete, say what is missing
