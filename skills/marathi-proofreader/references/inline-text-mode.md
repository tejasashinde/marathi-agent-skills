# MODE 1: Inline Text

## Step 1 — Content Isolation

Extract only the **Marathi text intended for proofreading**.

Ignore surrounding instructions or unrelated content.

If no text is provided, respond:
> No text was provided for Marathi proofreading. Please paste the Marathi content you would like reviewed.

---

## Step 2 — Error Detection Pass

Scan the Marathi text for the following issues:
- मराठी व्याकरणातील चुका  
- मराठी शुद्धलेखनातील चुका  
- मात्रा किंवा देवनागरी लेखनातील त्रुटी  
- विरामचिन्हांच्या चुका  
- अपूर्ण वाक्यरचना  
- खूप लांब किंवा जोडलेली वाक्ये  
- काळातील विसंगती  
- विभक्ती किंवा पूर्वप्रत्ययांचा चुकीचा वापर  
- सर्वनामातील अस्पष्टता  
- अनावश्यक पुनरावृत्ती  
- अडखळणारी किंवा अस्वाभाविक वाक्यरचना  
- विचारांच्या क्रमातील तुटकपणा  
- भाषाशैलीतील विसंगती  
- संज्ञा किंवा शब्दप्रयोगातील विसंगती  

---

## Step 3 — Voice Preservation Check (CRITICAL)

Before modifying any Marathi sentence, verify:
- Is the tone intentional?
- Is the conversational or informal style deliberate?
- Is repetition used for rhetorical effect?
- Is the fragmented structure stylistic?

If the sentence is **grammatically correct and intentional -> DO NOT CHANGE it.**

NEVER:
- Convert casual Marathi writing into formal Marathi without reason  
- Reduce emotional intensity of the original text  
- Replace distinctive or culturally meaningful vocabulary unnecessarily  
- Remove expressive or stylistic phrasing  

---

## Step 4 — Minimal Necessary Corrections

Apply only the **smallest correction necessary** to fix the issue.

Do not:

- Expand the content  
- Add new ideas or explanations  
- Remove subtle meaning or nuance  
- Rewrite entire paragraphs unless the structure is completely broken  

Prioritize **precision over stylistic preference**.

---

## Step 5 — Clarity & Flow Optimization

Where necessary:

- Break overly long or confusing Marathi sentences  
- Combine fragmented ideas where grammar requires it  
- Improve transitions between sentences or thoughts  
- Remove unnecessary repetition

The **original meaning and intent must remain unchanged.**

---

## Step 6 — Validation Pass

Before producing the final output, verify that:

- No Marathi grammar errors remain  
- Spelling and Devanagari writing are correct  
- The author's tone and writing style remain consistent  
- The meaning of the text has not changed  
- The author's stylistic identity has not been removed  
- Every correction is documented

If any issue is detected -> refine the corrections before producing the final output.
